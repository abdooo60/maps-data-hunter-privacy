Privacy Policy for Data Hunter Pro

Last updated: January 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Data Hunter Pro (“we”, “our”, or “the extension”) is a Chrome extension designed to help users organize and manage publicly available business information from web listings for research and productivity purposes.

We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how data is handled.

2. Information We Collect

Data Hunter Pro is designed to prioritize user privacy. We collect limited, non-personal data strictly necessary for the extension to function:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous statistics such as the number of items organized to enforce free usage limits.
  • Authentication Data: If applicable, a non-identifiable ID to track premium license status.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect or store:

  • Your name, email address (unless provided for support), or personal contact details.
  • Your browsing history or activity outside of the specific business listing pages.
  • Your credit card or financial information.
  • The business data you process: All organized business data remains on your local device and is never sent to our servers.

4. How We Use Information

Collected information is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the extension’s core functionality.
  • Validate premium subscription status.
  • Improve tool performance and user experience.

5. Data Storage and Sharing

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade user data with third parties.
  • All data processed from web pages is stored locally on the user's machine.

6. Permissions

Data Hunter Pro requests only the minimum permissions required to function. These permissions are used strictly to identify and format public information as requested by the user.
