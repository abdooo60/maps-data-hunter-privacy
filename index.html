Privacy Policy for Maps Data Hunter

Last updated: 24 December 2025

1. Introduction

Maps Data Hunter is a Chrome Extension designed to help users extract publicly available business information from Google Maps for research and productivity purposes.

2. Information We Collect

Maps Data Hunter does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data.

The extension only processes publicly available business information displayed on Google Maps, such as:

  • Business name
  • Address
  • Phone number
  • Website
  • Ratings and reviews

3. How We Use Information

All extracted data is processed locally within the user’s browser and is used only to display results and allow data export.

4. Data Storage and Sharing

  • No extracted data is stored on external servers
  • No data is sold or shared with third parties
  • The extension does not track users or browsing behavior

5. Permissions

Maps Data Hunter requests only the minimum permissions required to function properly, specifically to interact with Google Maps pages for data extraction.

6. Third-Party Services

This extension does not use analytics tools, advertising services, or third-party trackers.

7. Children’s Privacy

Maps Data Hunter is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
